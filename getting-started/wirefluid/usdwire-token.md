# $WIRE Token

**$WIRE** is the native utility and governance token of the WireFluid blockchain, powering transactions, security, and economic activity across the network. It is used to pay gas fees, stake with validators under the delegated proof-of-stake model, and secure instant-finality consensus. $WIRE also enables participation in on-chain governance, allowing holders to shape protocol upgrades and parameters. As the core economic asset, $WIRE aligns users, developers, creators, and validators within the WireFluid ecosystem.
